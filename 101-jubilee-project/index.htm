Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in the White Mountains of Arizona, with my family. My interest into tech began as I took my three youngest children to a coding class and was asked to stay as a parent volunteer. During the same time period, my older two boys' junior high and high school robotics teams competed at the World level. As I attended their competitions and classes, I decided I wanted to learn to code and began taking classes.

After my first class, I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build and create designs that would fill specific needs. I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch into front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
